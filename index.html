PhD Candidate, TU Delft, Web Information Systems Group

V Mourik Broekmanweg 6, 2628 XE, Delft, Netherlands

I am a PhD candidate at TU Delft in the Web Information Systems group. I’m working at the intersection of stream processing and serverless computing under the supervision of Asterios Katsifodimos.

Prior to that I obtained my MSc in Computer Science at TU Delft with a thesis at Holistic Schema Matching at Scale in collaboration with ING Netherlands. My first degree was a diploma (Integrated master’s degree) in Electrical and Computer Engineering at the Technical University of Crete.
